Women Composers (1800–1900) — Piano Works

Search and browse women composers born 1800–1900 with verifiable published piano music. Country grouping uses the historical country at birth (or the primary career country when more appropriate), normalized to common modern country names.

    Select a composer to view details.

    Inclusion criteria

    • Women composers born 1800–1900 (inclusive)
    • Includes composers with verifiable published piano music (score scan, edition listing, archive holding)
    • Works listed are limited to solo piano works

    Project files

    • composers.json — database
    • resources.md — research sources
    • by_country.md — generated country list

    Data lives in composers.json. Website is static: index.html + app.js + style.css.
